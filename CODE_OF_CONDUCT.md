# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Standards

**Expected behavior:**
- Be respectful and considerate
- Provide constructive feedback
- Focus on what is best for the community

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Trolling or sustained disruption
- Publishing others' private information

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive behavior may be reported to the project maintainer. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
